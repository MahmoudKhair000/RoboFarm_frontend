Welcome To RoboFarm

Intelligent Pest Detection and Data Insights for Palm Cultivation

RoboFarm is a state-of-the-art robotic system designed specifically for palm tree pest monitoring and analysis. Equipped with high-resolution cameras, environmental sensors, and AI-based image recognition, RoboFarm can autonomously navigate palm plantations, detect early signs of pest infestations such as red palm weevil or scale insects, and collect vital environmental data like temperature, humidity, and soil moisture.

Our system integrates seamlessly with a cloud-based platform that performs real-time data analysis and presents findings through clear, interactive visualizations. This empowers farmers, agricultural engineers, and researchers to monitor tree health remotely, track trends over time, and make fast, informed decisions to optimize crop protection and productivity.
